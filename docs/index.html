    About this showcase

    A machine for increasing the number of perspectives you can hold at once.

    Most platforms optimise for engagement and confirmation. Media Constellations does the opposite: it takes a piece of media and surfaces its underlying assumptions, the intellectual traditions that illuminate it, competing interpretations that disagree with each other, historical echoes, and — crucially — constructive things you can actually do. Not to tell you what to think, but to widen how many ways there are to think it.

    Tap a theme planet to open its thinkers and its competing readings. Tap a thinker to read why they belong here. Hit “perspectives I haven’t considered” to reveal what the media left out. The praxis dock turns critique into agency.

    This is a showcase: each constellation here was generated by an AI reasoning over the video’s transcript. The full tool maps any link you paste. Feedback very welcome.
